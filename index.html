About Biryani

Authentic Hyderabadi Biryani

"Biryani" is a mouthwatering dish. Famous in India, Pakistan and most of the Middle East. This authentic dish had been first introduced in Persia. The Mughal empire introduced it to India in 1398. It has blends of taste and health. Made with healthy spices, meat, Basmati Rice and cooked with a unique technique to preserve its nutritional values.
